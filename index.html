About Me

I’m an embedded software engineer with over a decade of experience in embedded systems and automotive software development. I currently lead the engineering department at Unique World Robotics (UWR), where I’m building a strong team of AI/ML and robotics engineers focused on innovative product development.

As the Co‑Founder of Cavira Innovations, I drive initiatives that merge embedded system design with real-world industrial applications, fostering innovation across technology and engineering domains.

Previously, I worked at Robert Bosch, contributing to Gateway ECU software for Daimler Trucks, Mercedes‑Benz, and Renault/Nissan platforms. Before that, at Ignitarium Technology Solutions, I collaborated with global clients including Nvidia, Renesas, and QNX on various embedded technology projects. My expertise spans embedded software and hardware design, with a particular focus on intelligent systems, robotics, and connected technologies.
